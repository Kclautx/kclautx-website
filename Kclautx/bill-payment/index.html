How long does bill payment take on KclautX?
Every bill payment on KclautX is processed instantly. Whether you're buying airtime, paying your electricity bill or renewing your cable TV subscription, confirmation comes through in seconds. What happens if my bill payment fails on KclautX?
Failed transactions are automatically reversed to your KclautX wallet immediately. You won't lose your money to a failed bill payment. However if you ever experience any issue with a bill payment, our support team is available 24/7 to resolve it. We take every failed transaction seriously because we know your bills can't wait.

Yes. KclautX supports electricity bill payment across major distribution companies in Nigeria including IKEDC for Lagos, EEDC for the Southeast, Kaduna Electric, PHCN Port Harcourt and more. You can pay your electricity bill regardless of which state you're in or which distributor serves your area. Simply select your distribution company, enter your meter number and pay instantly. We're continuously adding more electricity providers to ensure every Nigerian can handle their bill payment in one place.
